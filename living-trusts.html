Understanding Living Trusts in Virginia

As a Virginia estate planning attorney, I frequently advise clients about the benefits of living trusts. These powerful legal tools can help your family avoid the lengthy and expensive probate process while maintaining privacy.

Key Benefits

Faster Distribution

Assets can be distributed in weeks rather than months

Privacy Protection

Avoids public probate court records

Cost Effective

Reduces administrative fees and court costs

Common Questions

Do I still need a will with a living trust?

Yes, a "pour-over will" acts as a safety net for any assets not transferred to the trust.
