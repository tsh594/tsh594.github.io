Family Law FAQs

An uncontested divorce typically takes 2-3 months, while contested divorces can take 6 months to over a year depending on complexity.

Virginia courts consider the child's best interests, including each parent's ability to care for the child, the child's relationships, and stability factors.

Prenups are recommended if you have significant assets, own a business, have children from previous relationships, or want to protect family inheritances.
