Virginia's New DUI Laws in 2023

Virginia has implemented several changes to its DUI laws this year that every driver should be aware of. The most significant change is the reduction of the BAC threshold for enhanced penalties from 0.15% to 0.11%.

Key Changes Effective July 1, 2023

Previous Law New Law Impact
Enhanced penalties at 0.15% BAC Enhanced penalties at 0.11% BAC More drivers will face mandatory minimums
Ignition interlock required for 6 months Interlock required for 12 months Longer restriction period

Enhanced Penalties Now Include

  • Mandatory minimum 5 days jail time for first offense (previously no minimum)
  • Increased fines ($250-$500 additional)
  • Longer license suspension periods

What This Means for Drivers

These changes mean that:

  1. More drivers will qualify for enhanced penalties
  2. First-time offenders face stricter consequences
  3. Legal representation is more critical than ever
